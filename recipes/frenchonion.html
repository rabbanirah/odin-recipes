French Onion Soup

Home

Description

This is a French dish made with onions and beef broth.

Ingredients:

  • Onions
  • Olive oil
  • Unsalted butter
  • Provolone cheese
  • Parmesan chesse
  • French bread
  • Dried thyme
  • Sald and pepper
  • Dry sherry

Instructions:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Melt butter with olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onion to the pot and stir
  3. Add beef broth, sherry, salt, and pepper and let it simmir for 30 minutes. Preheat oven.
  4. Place soup into an oven-safe bowl with bread in each bowl. Add provolone cheese and parmesan on top of bread
  5. Place bowl in preheated oven for 3 minutes
  6. Serve dish!
