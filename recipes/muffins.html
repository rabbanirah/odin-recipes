Description

These muffins combined with carrots, apples, and cinnamon are tasty!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Walnuts
  • Carrots
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven and grease muffin tin
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, cinnamon, salt, sugar, apple, and carrot in a bowl
  3. In a separate bowl, mix eggs, oil, and vanilla extract
  4. Pour egg mixture into carrot mixture
  5. Place batter into muffin cups
  6. Bake in preheated oven for about 20 minutes
  7. Serve and enjoy!
