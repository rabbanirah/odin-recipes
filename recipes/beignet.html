Beignet

Home

Description

These beignets are squared shapped deep-fried pastry covered in powdered sugar.

Ingredients:

  • Warm water
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Unsalted butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Bread flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Oil

Directions:

  1. Preparations: Gather all ingredients.
  2. To make the dough: Mix water, yeast, and sugar in a bowl. Mix 2 large eggs with vanilla extract and milk in a separate bowl, then pour into the yeast mixture. Add butter, flour, and salt.
  3. Rest the dough: Beat until smooth. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  4. Shape the dough: Heat oil in a pan, remove the dough from the refrigerator and roll the dough out on a surface. Cut the dough into 2 squares.
  5. Fry the dough: Fry dough until it is golden. Transfer the beignet to a baking sheet.
  6. Serve: Add powdered sugar on top and serve!
